Changes


- General performance and optimization tweaks.
- Fixed a crash that occured whenever a fruit fell from a tree on a player's head.
- Added some new Chickens as well as fixed some of their recipes.
- General recipe and tooltips fixes.
- Added a new MineColonies style pack centered on reclaiming the Ancient City.
- Editing your keybinds should now be easier with the help of the Visual Keyboard.
- The Nature's Compass now costs XP and durability to prevent people from spamming it.
- You can now make plushies using the Sewing Machine.
